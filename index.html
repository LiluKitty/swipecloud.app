Frequently Asked Questions

Is SwipeCloud free?

Yes, SwipeCloud is completely free to download and use.

Back up photos, delete duplicates, and clean social media files without any cost.

Does SwipeCloud access my private photos?

No. Everything runs locally on your device. Our algorithms only analyze your media to find duplicates.

Your data never leaves your iPhone unless you upload to your own cloud accounts.

How does duplicate detection work?

SwipeCloud scans your library locally to find duplicate photos and videos, similar shots, blurry images, and photos with bad lighting.

Delete unwanted copies with one tap.

What is Social Clean?

Social Clean finds cached files from Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, and X.

These files accumulate on your iPhone without you knowing.

Is my data private?

Yes. SwipeCloud processes all data locally.

We never upload, store, or access your photos. Cloud backups go directly to your own accounts.

What is the Vault?

A secure, private space to hide sensitive photos and videos.

Files in the Vault are protected and hidden from your regular photo library.
